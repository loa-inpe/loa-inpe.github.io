Real-time oceanographic data - ATMOS and REMObs partnership

In this Antarctic summer of 2021/2022, the ATMOS projects (managed by the Laboratory for Ocean and Atmosphere Studies - LOA-OBT, from INPE) and REMObs (a partnership between the Navy Hydrography Center - CHM and PETROBRAS) came together to set up a complete monitoring system for the conditions of waves, winds and currents in the Antarctic region.

Data can be accessed in real-time through the page:http://www.operantar.live/

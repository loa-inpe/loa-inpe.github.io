Laboratory of Ocean and Atmosphere Studies

The Laboratory of Ocean and Atmosphere Studies (LOA), of the National Institute for Space Research (INPE), is part of the Earth Observation and Geoinformatics Division (DIOTG), linked to the Earth Observation Coordination (OBT/INPE) (CGCT) and is focused on the study of the ocean and atmosphere physics and the interaction between these two media, in addition to Biophysical Modeling.
